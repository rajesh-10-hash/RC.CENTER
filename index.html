Who We Are

A Community Built on Devotion & Friendship

RC Center is a passionate community friends group celebrating Vinayaka Chavithi with devotion, creativity, teamwork, and friendship since the year 2000.

For 25 years, from decoration planning to cultural events, every member contributes to create unforgettable memories for the entire community.

🎨

Creative Decorations

Stunning Ganesh pandal setups and lighting that dazzle the entire neighborhood.

🎭

Cultural Events

Music, dance and performances showcasing our rich cultural heritage.

🤝

Unity & Teamwork

Every member plays a vital role — we do everything together.

📸

Lasting Memories

We capture every precious moment in photos and videos forever.
