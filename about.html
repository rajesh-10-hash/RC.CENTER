Who We Are

A Community Built on Devotion & Friendship

RC Center is a passionate community friends group that comes together every year to celebrate Vinayaka Chavithi with devotion, creativity, teamwork, and friendship.

From decoration planning to cultural events, every member contributes their effort to create unforgettable memories for the community. We are united not just by tradition, but by the bonds we build with each other year after year.

🎨

Creative Decorations

Award-winning Ganesh pandal setups and vibrant lighting that light up the entire neighborhood.

🎭

Cultural Events

Music, dance, and performances that showcase the rich cultural heritage of our community.

🤝

Unity & Teamwork

Every member plays a role — from planning to execution, we do everything together.

📸

Lasting Memories

We capture every moment so that memories of our celebrations live forever in our gallery.

Our Mission

Bringing People Together Every Year

Our mission is simple — to celebrate the spirit of Vinayaka Chavithi in a way that brings joy to every family, builds lasting friendships, and keeps our cultural traditions alive for generations to come.
